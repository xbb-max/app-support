FlashDrama is a free app supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the app.

4.1 How Advertising Works

4.2 Your Advertising Choices

You can control personalized advertising on your device:

Note: Opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will see fewer ads, but the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.
