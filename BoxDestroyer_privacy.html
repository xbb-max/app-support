Privacy Policy

Box Destroyer Privacy Policy | Last Updated: 2024

1. Information Collection

We take your privacy seriously. When using this app, we may collect the following information:

  • Game progress and score data (stored locally)
  • Device information (for optimizing game experience)
  • Advertising identifiers (for displaying personalized ads)

All data is stored locally on your device and will not be uploaded to our servers.

2. Data Usage

We use the collected information for:

  • Saving your game progress

  • Providing personalized ad content (through third-party advertising services)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

This app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads: For displaying advertisements

These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for more information.

You can view Google's privacy policy at the following link:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption technology is used to protect sensitive data
  • Regular app updates to fix security issues

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear app data at any time
  • Opt out of personalized ads (through device settings)
  • Delete the app to remove all local data

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and know that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified to users within the app.

We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed about the latest privacy policy. Continued use of this app indicates your agreement to the updated privacy policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or complaints regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through:

  • In-app feedback feature
  • Developer email

We will respond to your inquiries within a reasonable time frame.
