Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Thank you for using Omni FM ("we", "our", or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Favorites/Bookmarks: Radio stations you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Playback History: Your listening history is stored locally for a better user experience.
  • Settings and Preferences: Your app settings (sleep timer, theme, etc.) are stored locally.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Radio Station Data: We fetch radio station information from public radio directories (RadioBrowser API).
  • App Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the app (crashes, feature usage).
  • Advertising Data: Our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain radio streaming services
  • To personalize your experience (favorites, recommendations)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To ensure content safety and compliance with app store policies

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data (favorites, history, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 RadioBrowser API

We use RadioBrowser (a community-driven radio station directory) to fetch station information. Their privacy policy is available at: radio-browser.info

4.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. You can learn more at: Google Privacy Policy

4.3 Radio Streaming Services

When you play a radio station, you connect directly to the station's streaming server. We do not control or have access to data collected by these servers.

5. Content Filtering

Important: For content safety and compliance with international regulations, we filter stations from certain high-risk regions where violent extremism and terrorism are prevalent. This filtering is based on objective security data and complies with:

  • Google Play Content Policy
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • International anti-terrorism regulations

This filtering does not represent any political stance and is solely for user safety.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the data stored locally in the app
  • Delete: Clear your favorites, history, and app data at any time through app settings or device settings
  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads through your device settings (Google Ad Settings / Apple Ad Tracking)

8. International Users

Omni FM is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of where you are located.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
